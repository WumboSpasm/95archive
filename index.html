More information
Archive95 is a search engine and viewer for internet material predating the Internet Archive's formation in 1996. Data is taken from these sources:
  • World Wide Web Directory by Jamsa Press - A website listing released in June 1995 as a book with a CD-ROM packed-in. The CD-ROM contains the markup of all ~8,000 pages listed in the book.
  • Einblicke ins Internet by Carl Hanser Verlag - A German-language CD released in October 1995 containing over 25,000 HTML, image and video files scraped from the web. It was meant to provide an offline internet experience for those who were not connected.
The viewer automatically updates pages to fix non-standard markup, reimplement unsupported browser features, and redirect links to archival sites.
